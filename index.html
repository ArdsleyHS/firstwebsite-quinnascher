Savory Waffles



Ingredients Amount Needed
All-Purpose Flour 2 Cups
Baking Powder 4 Teaspoons
Granulated Sugar 2 Tablespoons
Large Eggs 2
Warm Milk 1 1/2 Cups
Vanilla Extract 1 Teaspoon

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat the waffle iron
  2. In one large bowl, mix all the dry ingredients together
  3. In another large bowl, mix the wet ingredients
  4. Combine the dry and wet ingredients
  5. Spray the waffle iron and pour the mixure into the waffle iron
  6. Wait until it is a golden brown color
  7. Add any topping, and eat!

Mouth-Watering Crêpes



Ingredients Amount Needed
All-Purpose Flour 1 Cup
Kosher Salt 1/4 Teaspoon
Granulated Sugar 1 Tablespoon
Large Eggs 2
Whole Milk 1 1/2 Cups
Butter 1 Tablespoon

Instructions:

  1. In one large mixing bowl, mix the batter until it is smooth and thin
    • To mix you can use...
      • a hand mixer
      • a blender
      • a whisk
  2. Pour batter in a crêpes skillet or large pan using a ladle
  3. Move batter around the pan so it can maintain its smooth and thin consistency
  4. Flip it when needed and let it cook until it is golden brown
  5. Add any topping, and eat!

Yummy Banana Bread



Ingredients Amount Needed
All-Purpose Flour 2 Cups
Salt 1/4 Teaspoon
Brown Sugar 3/4 Cup
Large Eggs 2
Baking Soda 1 Teaspoon
Butter 1/2 Cup
Bananas 2 1/3 Cups

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. In one large bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt together
  3. In a different bowl, combine the butter and brown sugar
  4. Mash the bananas
  5. Mix the eggs with the mashed bananas
  6. Combine all three bowls together and stir
  7. Pour mixture into a pan and place it into the oven
  8. Let it cook for 60-65 minutes
  9. Eat!
