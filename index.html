  • Savory Waffles
    • Ingredients
      1. 2 Cups of all-Purpose Flour
      2. 1 Teaspoon of Salt
      3. 4 teaspoons baking powder
      4. 2 Tablespoons of White Sugar
      5. 2 Large Eggs
      6. 1 1/2 Cups of Warm Milk
      7. 1/3 Cups of Melted Butter
      8. 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Exstract
    • Steps
      1. Mix the flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a big bowl.
      2. Mix the milk, butter, and vanilla together in a separate bowl.
      3. Crack the eggs in bowl containing the milk-mixture; gently stir together.
      4. Mix dry and wet ingredients.
      5. Poor batter in a pre-heated waffle iron; take the waffle out when it is a golden brown.
